About

Irbis Lab is an Argentinian software development company founded in 2012. Our mission is to craft through hard-work great web and mobile apps focusing on simplicity and creativity. We pursue excellence and not only our clients’ happiness, but also ours.

We are a small team of developers who are passionate about creating great products. Our entrepreneurial spirit leads us to be constantly nurturing ideas, either for profit or fun.

We work along entrepreneurs and companies alike, to turn their ideas into outstanding products.
