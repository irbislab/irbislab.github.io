Work

Our Process

Discovery and Plan

We begin defining the scope of your project and understanding your business objectives. After performing a broad requirements gathering, we discuss about the product design and the right architecture. After that, we’ll provide a preliminary budget and project timeline.

Design

We seriously believe that design is a critical part of each project. Our main concern in this stage is to have the simplest and most efficient solution.

Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works

— Steve Jobs

We work side by side with design studio Esmile to create appealing product identities.

Development

As the final product is more important than extensive documentation, our development process is based on Agile methodologies, such as TDD (Test-driven development), which aim at having projects be broken down into small, deliverable cycles. Each cycle starts defining the functionality to be implemented and finishes once all the features have been tested successfully.

It’s worth noting our preference for lightweight tools over giant, monolithic ones. We are convinced that these flexible and simple tools let you focus on the concrete problem domain to solve, boosting efficiency and code reliability. Some of the tools we work with are: Sinatra for Ruby, Flask for Python and Node.js. We also like to use Redis on some of our projects.

Deployment

Your product will be ready to reach the targeted users after we set up your deployment environment. We will handle the gritty configuration details so you can focus on what’s important to you: your business and clients. We can also help you out with social media and overall web presence.

Future

There is no last step in this whole process... we believe in long lasting business relationships, so we will always be available in the future to help you find new opportunities or develop new features that improve your products.
