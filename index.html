About

Irbis Lab is an Argentinian software development company founded in 2012. Our mission is to craft through hard-work great web and mobile apps focusing on simplicity and creativity. We pursue excellence and not only our clients’ happiness, but also ours.

Services

We provide integrated solutions, Mobile and Cloud Development, including full integrations with third party services, such as Twitter and Facebook.

Mobile Applications

Mobile is growing fast and with that comes new business opportunities. We create web and iOS native applications.

Cloud Development

We use the latest tools and practices that allow for the rapid development of scalable systems.

Own Projects

Our entrepreneurial spirit leads us to be constantly nurturing ideas. We like to design and build our own products. We love open-source projects.

Our Process

With Irbis Lab, you are choosing to work with us as a team. We works with you from business & product strategy to deployment and first clients interactions.

Research & Discovery

We begin defining the scope of your project and understanding your business objectives. After performing a broad requirements gathering, we discuss about the product design and the right architecture.

Design

We seriously believe that design is a critical part of each project. Our main concern in this stage is to have the simplest and most efficient solution.

Development

Our development process is based on Agile methodologies, such as TDD (Test-driven development), which aim at having projects be broken down into small, deliverable cycles (Sprints). Features are delivered incrementally.

Deployment

Your product will be ready to reach the targeted users after we set up your deployment environment. We will handle the gritty configuration details so you can focus on what’s important to you: your business and clients.

We believe in long lasting business relationships, so we will always be available in the future to help you find new opportunities or develop new features that improve your products.

Our Tools

It’s worth noting our preference for lightweight tools over giant, monolithic ones. We are convinced that simple tools let you focus on the concrete problem domain to solve, boosting efficiency and code reliability. Some of the tools we work with are: Sinatra, Flask and Node.js. We also like to use Redis and MongoDB.
